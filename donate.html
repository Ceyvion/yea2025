Special report

Soundtrack of resilience

Producer Lamine “Leo” Fofana returned to his hometown of Conakry this year to document how music collectives kept their communities connected through upheaval. His four-part series blends first-person storytelling with field recordings that haven’t been heard beyond Guinea—yet.

Your donation unlocks the resources to finish Leo’s story, preserve fragile archives, and share them with our million-listener community. Give today to keep these sounds alive.

Where your money goes

  • 45%
    Field production

    Travel stipends, local fixers, and equipment upkeep for on-the-ground storytelling.

  • 30%
    Archival preservation

    Digitizing rare recordings and maintaining our public access archive.

  • 15%
    Training & fellowships

    Mentoring emerging producers across Africa and the diaspora.

  • 10%
    Community access

    Free educational toolkits, live listening sessions, and school programs.

Questions & answers

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Afropop Worldwide is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Your gift is tax-deductible to the fullest extent of the law.

Can I donate by check or stock transfer?

Yes. Contact us at development@afropop.org for instructions. We welcome DAF and IRA gifts too.

How do I become a sustaining member?

Select “Monthly” as your gift type above or email us for custom pledge amounts. Sustaining donors receive quarterly studio briefings.
